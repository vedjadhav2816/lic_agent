Frequently Asked Questions

How can I become an LIC agent?

You can register through our online portal or visit your nearest LIC branch. Complete the registration process and attend the required training.

What are the working hours?

As an LIC agent, you have flexible working hours. You can manage your schedule according to your convenience.

How long does it take to get a response?

Our team typically responds to inquiries within 24-48 business hours.
